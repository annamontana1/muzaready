## üìû QUESTIONS & CLARIFICATIONS

**Q: Why is URL structure critical before Priority 3?**
A: Once catalog is live, changing URLs requires 301 redirects (complexity). Finalizing before saves rework.

**Q: What if we publish slower than 2-3 articles per week?**
A: Growth will be slower but still positive. Adjust targets downward in metrics dashboard.

**Q: Can we start with Priority 2 (payments) before Priority 1 (cart)?**
A: No, Priority 1 is foundation. Priority 2 depends on Priority 1 being complete.

**Q: What if keyword rankings don't match timeline?**
A: Normal variance. Content takes 2-4 weeks to index, 4-12 weeks to rank. Monitor trends, not daily.

**Q: How do we know if backlink strategy is working?**
A: Track in Ahrefs/Semrush monthly. Target 5+ backlinks by week 4, 75+ by week 10.

**Q: Should we hire a freelance writer or use internal resources?**
A: Freelance = $300/article (15 articles = $4.5K). Internal = salary (consider opportunity cost).
